Discovery Session

This in-depth 2-hour session requires you to do “prep work” ahead of time, including a questionnaire and a worksheet.

In your Discovery Session we’ll discuss your financial situation and recommended action steps for moving forward to your goals.

Your investment for this is $255. This session may be all you need to get yourself going in the direction you want!

How It Works

  1. Usually we have a Q&A Call before this. But feel free to jump right in and schedule a Discovery Session if you’d like (you’ll need to pay at the time of scheduling).
  2. You’ll receive an email confirmation that gives you your prep work and when it is due. Your confirmation will also include a Zoom meeting room that is set up for our session.
  3. You do your work and submit it electronically.
  4. I’ll contact you to confirm I’ve received your prep work.
  5. We meet on Zoom at our appointed time. Allow 2 hours.
  6. You’ll receive a Recap of your Discovery Session and recommended steps to move forward to your goals.
